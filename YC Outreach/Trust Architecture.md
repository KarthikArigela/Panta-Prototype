3. Asymmetric Commitment / Skin in the Game
Why it works from first principles:
When you have no reputation, you have one asset: your ability to absorb risk.
By offering asymmetric terms, you:

Signal confidence: You're betting on yourself
Reverse the power dynamic: You're offering value, not asking for it
